    This is an example of searching for and enzyme on the 2Path database.



    1. Go to search page, select an organism and click on "Search Enzyme".


    2. Give the EC number of the enzyme, say 2.7.7.60, and click on "Search". If the enzyme is there, you can find details about it on the graph at the right. You can find details about the nodes by hovering the mouse over them.
