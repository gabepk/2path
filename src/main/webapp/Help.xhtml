    This is an example of searching for and enzyme on the 2Path database.



    1. Go to search page and click on "Search Enzyme".


    2. Give the EC number of the enzyme, say 2.7.7.60, and click on "Search". If the enzyme is there, you can find details about it by clicking on "View interactive data".


    3. See the reactions this enzyme catalises and manipulate the pathway as you wish. The Labels sections tells you the meaning of each node and edge on the pathway
